# TYMIT EMPLOYEE HANDBOOK

## 1. REMOTE WORK POLICY
At Tymit, we believe in flexibility. However, to ensure collaboration:
- Employees are allowed to work remotely up to 2 days per week.
- Wednesdays are mandatory in-office days for all teams.
- Any remote work request must be submitted 48 hours in advance via the HR portal.
- Working from abroad is strictly prohibited due to tax implications, unless approved by the CEO directly.
- Core hours are 10:00 AM to 4:00 PM UK time. You must be available on Slack during these hours.

## 2. PAID TIME OFF (PTO)
We offer a competitive leave package:
- 25 days of annual leave per year, plus bank holidays.
- Unused leave cannot be carried over to the next year.
- You must book leave at least 2 weeks in advance for any duration longer than 3 days.
- Sick leave is paid at 100% for the first 5 days. Afterwards, Statutory Sick Pay (SSP) applies.
- Emergency leave is available for family emergencies (up to 3 days per year).

## 3. EXPENSE POLICY
We trust our employees to spend company money wisely.
- Travel expenses must be pre-approved for amounts over £100.
- Team lunches are capped at £25 per person.
- Hardware budget: New joiners receive a £2000 budget for a laptop and accessories.
- Home office allowance: £200 one-off payment for a desk/chair setup.
- All receipts must be uploaded to Expensify within 30 days.

## 4. SECURITY & DATA PROTECTION
Security is everyone's responsibility.
- Do not share passwords via Slack or email. Use 1Password.
- Lock your screen whenever you leave your desk.
- Data classification:
  - PUBLIC: Marketing materials.
  - INTERNAL: General company docs.
  - CONFIDENTIAL: Customer data, financial reports.
- Data Leakage Prevention: Do not upload customer data to public AI tools (e.g., ChatGPT free version).
- Incident Reporting: Report any suspected breach to security@tymit.com immediately.

## 5. PROBATION PERIOD
- The standard probation period is 6 months.
- Reviews happen at 3 months and 5 months.
- Notice period during probation is 1 week.
- Notice period after probation is 2 months.

## 6. PROMOTION & REVIEWS
- Performance reviews occur bi-annually in June and December.
- Promotion cycles are aligned with the December review.
- Salary adjustments are effective from January 1st.

## 7. IT & EQUIPMENT USE
All company-issued equipment remains the property of Tymit.
- Laptop Refresh Cycle: Engineering laptops are replaced every 3 years. MacBooks are standard.
- Software Installation: You do not have admin rights by default. Request access via JIRA (ticket type: IT-ACCESS).
- Personal Use: Occasional personal use is permitted, but do not store personal photos or sensitive documents on company drives.
- Lost Equipment: Must be reported within 2 hours to IT Support.
- Returns: Upon leaving, all equipment must be returned to the London office within 3 days.

## 8. CODE OF CONDUCT
We value respect and integrity.
- Harassment: Zero tolerance policy. Report to HR immediately.
- Conflict of Interest: You cannot hold a second job or consultancy role without written approval.
- Social Media: Do not post confidential roadmap info on LinkedIn/Twitter. "Views are my own" does not absolve you of NDA breaches.
- Dress Code: Casual, but smart-casual for client meetings.

## 9. PARENTAL LEAVE
Supporting our families is key.
- Maternity: 16 weeks full pay, followed by statutory.
- Paternity: 4 weeks full pay.
- Adoption: Mirrors maternity policy.
- Return to Work: Phased return (3 days/week) available for the first month back at full pay.

## 10. MENTAL HEALTH & WELLBEING
- Headspace Subscription: Free for all employees.
- Counseling: 5 free sessions per year via our provider, Spill.
- Mental Health Days: 2 days per year, no questions asked (separate from sick leave).

## 11. REFERRAL PROGRAM
We love hiring friends of Tymit.
- Bonus: £1,500 for non-tech roles, £2,500 for tech roles.
- Payment Terms: Paid after the referred employee passes probation (6 months).
- Eligibility: Hiring managers cannot refer for their own open roles.

## 12. TRAVEL & ACCOMMODATION
When traveling for business:
- Flights: Economy class for flights under 6 hours. Premium Economy allowed for longer.
- Hotels: Max £150/night in London, £200/night in major global cities (NY, SF).
- Per Diem: £50/day for meals when traveling overnight.

## 13. LEARNING & DEVELOPMENT (L&D)
- Budget: £1000 per year per employee.
- Use Case: Conferences, Books, Online Courses (Coursera, Udemy).
- Approval: Manager approval required.
- Study Leave: 2 days per year for exam preparation or conference attendance.

## 14. GRIEVANCE PROCEDURE
If you have an issue:
1. Speak to your line manager first.
2. If unresolved, escalate to the Head of People.
3. Formal written grievance triggers an investigation within 5 working days.

## 15. WHISTLEBLOWING
We protect those who speak up.
- Report unethical behavior anonymously via the 'SafeCall' hotline.
- No retaliation will be tolerated against whistleblowers.
- Covered issues: Fraud, bribery, danger to health/safety.
